Frequently asked questions

How do I upload task details and relevant pictures?

As a Customer, you can upload task details and relevant pictures directly from your dashboard. Simply navigate to the "Upload Task" section, fill in the required details, and upload any relevant pictures.

How do I initiate a task invitation for Taskers to accept the task?

As a Customer, you can initiate a task invitation by selecting the "Invite Taskers" option from your dashboard. Fill in the required details about the task, specify the necessary skills, and send out invitations to Taskers who match your criteria.

How do I view the total tasks completed and profit earned?

As a Tasker, you can view the total tasks completed and profit earned by accessing the "Task History" section from your dashboard. Here, you'll find detailed information about each completed task, including the profit earned.

How do I manage newly invited, scheduled, ongoing, and past tasks?

As a Tasker, you can manage your tasks by accessing the "Task Management" section from your dashboard. Here, you'll find options to view newly invited, scheduled, ongoing, and past tasks. You can also update task statuses and communicate with Customers.
