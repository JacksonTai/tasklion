Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

How do I pay for the services?

You will pay your Tasker directly in cash based on the quotation they provide. We recommend keeping a record of your cash payment and asking your Tasker for a receipt or confirmation of payment for your records.

How can I become a Tasker?

To become a Tasker, you need to sign up for a Tasklion Account. Once you have an account, you can setup a tasker profile and start offering your services to customers.

What is a Tasklion Account?

Your Tasklion Account is your universal login for everything Tasklion has to offer! Whether you want to get things done as a customer or offer your skills as a tasker, one account is all you need.

How do I contact my taskers or customers?

You can contact your taskers or customers via the provided email address or phone number associated with their Tasklion account. This information is available in the task details section.

How do I manage my tasks?

As a Tasker, you can manage your tasks by accessing the "Task" section from your dashboard. Here, you'll find options to view tasks in different statuses.
